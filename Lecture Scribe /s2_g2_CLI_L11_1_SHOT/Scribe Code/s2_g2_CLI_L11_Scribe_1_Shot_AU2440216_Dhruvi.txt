\section{Proofs or Derivations}

\subsection*{Transformation of a Random Variable}

Let:
\[
Y = g(X)
\]

Then:
\[
F_Y(y) = P(Y \le y) = P(g(X) \le y).
\]

Depending on the nature of $g(\cdot)$, this probability is expressed in terms of
$X$ and evaluated using the known distribution of $X$, as shown step-by-step
in the slides.
